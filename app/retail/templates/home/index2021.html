Why contribute to Science?

The pursuit of knowledge is one of the important endeavors that we are undertaking. By sharing this knowledge openly and utilizing decentralized blockchains to further dissemination and creation of knowledge, we aim to create a new process for everyone to get involved.

Open

Open source code meets open economies. Build resilient projects, better coordination, and positive-sum outcomes.

Empowering

Growing networks with aligned incentives towards the wellbeing of each participant and the system as a whole.

Collaborative

Through distributed funding and organizations, we build together toward our shared goals.

Empowering the next generation of scientists

A significant number of PhDs will graduate without a job offer awaiting them in academia or industry. This leaves a bleak outlook for their career prospects. We seek to empower people with specialized knowledge to continue contributing to scientific research in a more dynamic way.
